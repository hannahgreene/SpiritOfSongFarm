Meet the Owners

Moo and Andy Greene are the owners of Spirit of Song. In October of 2018, they celebrated their 30th wedding anniversary. Moo is a long-time horse lover and has been living out her childhood dream of owning horses at Sprit of Song Farm. She is a violin teacher, lawyer, singer, and mother to three beautiful daughters. Andy, a professor of biomedical engineering, has grown to love the horses and has loved every minute of taking care of the horses because he knows it makes his wife happy. While not doing science, he is a master craftsman, cook, and the father of three incredible daughters. They are both lovers of dogs, good food, and exotic travel.
